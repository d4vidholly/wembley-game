Cup Heroes

How this works vs. the board game

In the Wembley Companion, Cup Heroes replace International Stars listed on the board game. Just like International Stars, physical Cup Heroes tokens are purchased before a match. If they are selected and finish on the winning team they are kept by the winning manager. If they lose they are returned for any player to purchase.

New mechanics have been added to make this a more immersive and realistic gaming experience, reflecting the real-world FA Cup heroics of legends who made Wembley their stage.
